Welcome to MVLAMUN II

The confrence will be held in Palo Alto, near Gunn High School on Sunday, May 15th. Catered food will be provided by Buca di Beppo. The fee for the confrence is $20, payable by check, or online through PayPal.

MVLAMUN II will be a joint crisis committee, set in the Spanish Civil War. The committees will be the Nationalists, and the Republicans. Position papers will be due on May 13th at 11:59 PM. A tennative schedule can be found here.

Questions? Contact Andreas Godderis, Republican head chair, Jenna Goosen, Nationalist head chair, or Jasper Fung.
